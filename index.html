Profile

I work at the point where strategy meets delivery — helping governments and large organizations design what they want to become, then build the operating model that gets them there.

FocusAI · Digital · Strategy · Operating models
SectorsGovernment, Tech, Health, Financial Services
RegionUnited Arab Emirates · GCC
BasedDubai, UAE

Over more than sixteen years across strategy consulting and public-sector advisory, I have led national digital platforms, stood up new government entities end to end, and embedded AI and value-realization frameworks that tie transformation back to measurable outcomes.

My approach is structured and methodology-led, but the test is always operational: a clear mandate, an org and operating model that works on the ground, and KPIs that prove value was realized, not just promised.

I am currently focused on senior strategy and transformation leadership, partnering with executive teams shaping technology and AI-led change across the region.
